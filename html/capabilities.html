Engineering Capabilities

Architectural Engineering

Involved detail design of various types of infrastructure projects such as Villas, Hotels, office buildings, commercial complexes, hospitals and ancillary buildings, schools, communication facilities, Control buildings, Workshops and maintenance facilities, pumping stations, housing complex and mosques.

The process involves initial conceptual architectural design approval by the client and inalization of architectural layouts, elevations, cross sections, perspectives, material specifications and preparation of tender drawings in coordination with Civil, Structural, and Mechanical, Piping, Electrical and Instrumentation engineering discipline.

Civil/Structural Engineering

GED Consultants involved in successful completion of various civil and structural projects designed, utilizing the most cost-effective structures and carefully tailored to suit individual project requirement such as schedule and budget requirements. These structures include reinforced concrete, pre-cast concrete\pre-stressed concrete, steel structures, plate and shell design.

The civil & structural group performs detail design of the following areas. These areas are as Follows:

a) Process Plant Support Structures: Design of special structures such as pipe supports, Thrust Anchors, pipe racks, bridges, equipment foundation, underground infrastructure such as pumps, tanks, valve boxes, Storm & Oily water drainage systems.

b) Industrial Plant Support Structures: Provide more independent designs of specialized plant structures such as storage tanks & silos, equipment mounting structures, platforms, walkways, Overhead crane structures and roadways etc.
