Engineering Capabilities

Architectural Engineering

Involved detail design of various types of infrastructure projects such as Villas, Hotels, office buildings, commercial complexes, hospitals and ancillary buildings, schools, communication facilities, Control buildings, Workshops and maintenance facilities, pumping stations, housing complex and mosques.

The process involves initial conceptual architectural design approval by the client and inalization of architectural layouts, elevations, cross sections, perspectives, material specifications and preparation of tender drawings in coordination with Civil, Structural, and Mechanical, Piping, Electrical and Instrumentation engineering discipline.

Civil/Structural Engineering

GED Consultants involved in successful completion of various civil and structural projects designed, utilizing the most cost-effective structures and carefully tailored to suit individual project requirement such as schedule and budget requirements. These structures include reinforced concrete, pre-cast concrete\pre-stressed concrete, steel structures, plate and shell design.

The civil & structural group performs detail design of the following areas. These areas are as Follows:

a) Process Plant Support Structures: Design of special structures such as pipe supports, Thrust Anchors, pipe racks, bridges, equipment foundation, underground infrastructure such as pumps, tanks, valve boxes, Storm & Oily water drainage systems.

b) Industrial Plant Support Structures: Provide more independent designs of specialized plant structures such as storage tanks & silos, equipment mounting structures, platforms, walkways, Overhead crane structures and roadways etc.

Piping System Design:

Piping System Design: We design piping systems as per API, ASME and NEMA codes and based on good engineering practices. These include preparation of plan and profiles of cross country pipelines, piping arrangement drawings, piping isometrics and specifying all materials for procurement.

The piping designs are carefully analyzed statically or dynamically using CAESAR II stress analysis software. We also perform checking of vendor data for technical suitability, preparation of piping production drawings, material control, stress and flexibility analysis, plant equipment layout drawings, installation drawings and specifications etc.

GED consultants has also furnished Oil & gas piping, crude stabilizers utilities and power piping, steam, water distribution networks, compressed air and other gas systems, firewater, fire protection systems, plumbing, chilled water systems, industrial and sanitary sewer pipeline networks, oily and storm water drainage network system.

Preparation of valve data sheets, Safety instruction sheets (SIS), orifice plates, pressure safety valves specification etc., and bills of materials.

Stress analysis: Our piping designs assure adequate flexibility, and specify the details of piping loops, pipe supports include rigid supports (shoes, dummy legs, trunnions, low friction sliders, etc.) and spring supports (variable rate and constant foremen).

Electrical:

The company has a track record of producing innovative and cost effective electrical system designs, covering switchgear, transformers, UPS, capacitor bank, lighting, cable & conduit sizing and also grounding calculations. GED consultants has in-house capability to perform a range of computer simulations of power systems including short circuit, load flow, relay setting and fault level studies using ETAP software, as well as the design of electrical protection systems. The electrical team is expert in developing specifications, data sheets of various electrical equipment's and detail drawings such as single line drawing, electrical area classification drawings, power & grounding layout, lighting layout, electrical equipment layout, wiring diagram, cable & conduit schedule.

Instrumentation:

The instrumentation teams provide advanced, modernized instrumentation and control/monitoring system to various industries process system. Our team develop design for Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA), Distributed Control Systems (DCS), Emergency Shutdown System (ESD), Vibration monitoring system (VMS), Flow Level, Pressure Temperature and Density Measurement System for Process Industries Flow metering, protective instrumentation, Fire Detection and Gas Monitoring Systems, Safety Interlocks, Sequencing Control and Start-up Shutdown Systems, process analyzers such as gas chromatographs, environmental process monitors, and on-line process fluid property analyzers.

The instrumentation team is expert in developing functional specification (FSD) and other details such as instrumentation block diagrams, Instrument Data Sheets, Instrument Loop Diagrams, Control Logic Diagrams, Control Valve Sizing and Specifications, Wiring Diagrams, Installation Details, cable & conduit layout.
